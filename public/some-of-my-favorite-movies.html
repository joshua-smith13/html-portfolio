Some of my Favorite Movies

Not necessarily top 4, but movies I enjoy.

The Princess Bride

I really enjoyed this movie. The Acting was beautifully done, and the comedic beat was wonderful.

Howl's Moving Castle

Grew up watching this as one of my first anime movies. Studio Ghibili made this beautiful piece of a movie.

Space Balls

A Comedy poking fun of Star Wars. I really enjoyed it, though I don't know to describe it was funny to me. It has Mel Brooks in it.

The Hunt for Red October

This is a nice action packed movie. It's a bit older than recent movie's but I very much enjoy it still. You have Sean Connery, Alec Baldwin, Sam Neill, Tim Curry, and James Earl Jones.
